I honestly didn’t expect to find seafood this good inside a food court. 😮🇹🇭

If you’re eating at Siam Paragon, don’t skip KinKin Seafood. We tried the Millionaire Crab Fried Rice, Premium Crab Curry Rice, Fried Mantis Shrimp with Chili and Garlic, Deep Fried Sea Bass, and Mini Tom Yum Noodle, and every dish was worth trying.

💡 My tip: You can order à la carte or as a set. I’d recommend the set because you’ll get to try more dishes while saving a bit.
