The ultimate in world-class relaxation, ‘The Unbound Collection by Hyatt’ debuts its first hotel in Thailand: ‘THE BARAI HUA HIN’ (@thebaraihuahin). This boutique wellness destination has evolved from one of Thailand’s most acclaimed spas, backed by over 20 years of health and wellness expertise. Now transformed into a full-scale boutique resort, it offers an elevated escape on the serene Khao Takiab beachfront in Hua Hin. The resort features a unique design inspired by the four elements—water, fire, earth, and air—seamlessly blending architecture, nature, and the distinctive cultural heritage of the Thai way of life.
