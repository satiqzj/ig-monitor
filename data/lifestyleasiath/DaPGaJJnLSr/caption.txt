For the first time in the Open Era, two Thai players have won Grand Slam main-draw matches at the same tournament, and both are through to round 2 of Wimbledon 2026. Mananchaya Sawangkaew pulled off a stunning comeback against Maja Chwalinska, rallying from 2-0 down in the final set to win 2-6, 7-5, 6-2. Meanwhile, Lanlana Tararudee held off Lilli Tagger 7-6(3), 5-7, 6-4, surviving a fightback from 5-1 up in the decider. Eighteen years after Tanasugarn's quarterfinal run in 2008, Thai tennis is writing a new chapter on the grass. We’re rooting for them for round 2!
