This October, Bangkok’s acoustic music community will come together once again as Southeastern Gathering returns for its 2026 edition. Taking place from 20–25 October at Public House Bangkok, the six-day festival celebrates bluegrass, Irish traditional, and old-time music through live performances, workshops, jam sessions, and storytelling events.
