French singer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist FKJ (@frenchkiwijuice) is coming to Bangkok this November. Promoting his third studio album, ‘Tyber,’ the artist will be performing across Asia, including stops in Singapore Kuala Lumpur, Hong Kong, Seoul, Taipei, and Tokyo. The Bangkok concert is the first on the Asia tour, set for 27 November 2026 at UOBB Live, Emsphere.
