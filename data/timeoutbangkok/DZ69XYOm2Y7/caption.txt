Grab your tote and clear an afternoon: TCDC's 'Stationerycore: A Love Affair with Stationery' is a paper-lover's playground ✏️📓 
Wander through the world of notebooks, pens, paper, textures and clever Thai design, then get hands-on at the Material Try-On Zone, where you can scribble, test and handle different papers, colours, textures and materials. The World-Class Design Awards Zone also unpacks globally recognised stationery pieces, from function and innovation to good looks ✨ 

Catch it at the Creative Space, 5th Floor, TCDC Bangkok, Bang Rak. Free entry, July 1-October 18, 10.30am-7pm, closed Mondays.
