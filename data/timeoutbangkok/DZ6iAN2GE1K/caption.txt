When the light of imagination blossoms as art ✨🌸

The Lantern Art Festival glows back to life, and this year it goes by the theme 'The Luminous Bloom'. Picture the heart of the city reborn as a breathtaking garden of light, where towering installations open up like flowers after dark 🏮

Wander among a dazzling collection of handcrafted lanterns, each one bathed in vibrant colour and a warm, golden gleam. The whole place hums with magic, paths lined with glittering petals, sculptures that shimmer and shift and little pockets of wonder tucked around every corner.

At the Plaza in front of @samyanmitrtown, July 17-31. Free of charge.
