Trade your usual weekend plans for a floating arts party on the Chao Phraya River 🚢🎨

Wander gallery-style exhibitions, snap up handmade treasures and get stuck in at hands-on workshops, then park yourself riverside and let that breeze do its thing. Come sundown, climb to the boat's rooftop deck where live tunes light up the waterfront.

You catch it all at Bangkok Island Pier, Rama 3 Soi 64, on July 5 from 4pm-11.59pm. Entry stays free for everyone.
