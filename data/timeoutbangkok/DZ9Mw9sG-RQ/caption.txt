Artful Intimacy at ANIMA Saigon

Just a short flight from Thailand – direct flights from Bangkok are plentiful and inexpensive – Vietnam’s vibrant Ho Chi Minh City is a great option when looking for a convenient R&R fix with a cultural difference. 

Those with an artistic inclination in particular will love a few days at ANIMA Saigon Boutique Hotel (@animasaigon), where a setting inspired by contemporary Vietnamese art helps guests re-connect and revitalise before embarking on new adventures. The 32-room urban hide-away invites you to experience central Saigon from a more intimate perspective. Here wi-fi enabled and air-conditioned accommodation has been decorated by young Vietnamese artists who paint bold portraits of contemporary Vietnam.

When not luxuriating in your room or out exploring the locale via the hotel’s flexible shuttle service, you can unwind at the swimming pool and spa—the steam room being a favourite—or fit in a cardio session at the modern gym, which helps to work up an appetite for drinking in the delights at ÚNU Eatery & Bar. A dining destination in itself, ÚNU Eatery & Bar is where cuisine, cocktails, art and emotions come together in one shared experience. 

📍ANIMA Saigon Boutique Hotel, 43-45 Ho Tung Mau Street, Sai Gon Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
