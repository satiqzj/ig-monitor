@yonlapa rank among Thailand's most beloved bands, and the Chiang Mai dream-pop crew keep racking up tour dates at home and across Asia, winning a devoted following with their hazy, atmospheric sound 🎸

Fresh from a run that wrapped in May, the group finally bring fans the news they crave: a first full-scale solo show in Bangkok. Titled 'Fungjai SOLO Series 02: YONLAPA Homebound', the gig promises something audiences haven't witnessed before – though the band stay tight-lipped on the finer details for now.

Catch them on August 28 at @volumelivehouse, fifth floor of The Street Ratchada. Tickets on sale from June 26 🎟️
