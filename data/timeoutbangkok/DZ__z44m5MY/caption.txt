House Samyan turns 22, and the party runs all July long 🎥 

The Bangkok cinema marks its anniversary with a month of special activities, hand-picked film programmes and a few cheeky surprises for movie lovers. The headliner? House Classics, which brings Wes Anderson's deadpan masterpiece The Royal Tenenbaums (2001) back to the big screen. 🎉

Whether you already adore Anderson's symmetrical, pastel-soaked worlds or you're meeting the Tenenbaum clan for the very first time, catching this one properly, darkened room, popcorn in hand. 

Plenty more lands across the month too, from quality films to exclusive events. Follow @house_samyan for the full line-up 🍿
