See you on the dancefloor at @siamsquareofficial this Friday 💃?

Bangkok's iconic youth district is launching a new monthly street dance night, and the debut is going full Pride mode: outdoor vogue classes, ballroom energy, a Thailand Ballroom Girlies performance and a giant rainbow LED screen lighting up Walking Street.

Behind it is PMCU, the team behind the old Siam Square World Dance Carnival, now back with something built to last: a free public dancefloor every second Friday of the month, with a new theme each time.

Never vogued before? rumPUREE – World Dance Studio has beginner classes on the night.

Outfit optional. Vogue face strongly encouraged 💅

📍 Walking Street, Siam Square
 📅 Friday June 27 
 ⏰ From 6pm
 🎟️ Free
 🚆 BTS Siam (exits 2 or 4), two-minute walk
