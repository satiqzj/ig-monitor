Pak Khlong Talat does flowers better than anywhere else in Bangkok, but this weekend its famous blooms get unexpected company 🌸Bangkok Art Walk returns to the riverside market from June 26-28.

More than 50 Thai and international artists set up along the petal-strewn lanes, showing off paintings, photography and a heap of handmade crafts. Live music drifts through the stalls, mingling with marigolds and jasmine, while the market hustle takes on a creative edge. 

Bangkok Art Walk runs daily from June 26-June 28, 2pm-11pm, with parking sorted at Talat Yot Phiman Market.
