Pick your leader with a clear head, Bangkok 🗳️ 

Just as Thailand scraps the afternoon drinking ban, Bangkok gets one more dry spell for election day. 

No alcohol can be sold or served anywhere in the city from 6pm on June 27 to 6pm on June 28. That means bars, restaurants, clubs, convenience stores and supermarkets are all covered.

Break the rule and you could face up to six months in jail, a B10,000 fine, or both. 

Do your civic duty, then cheers after 6pm 🍻
