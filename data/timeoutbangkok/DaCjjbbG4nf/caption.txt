Clear a little space in your wardrobe and make a beeline for Whatever You Art 🛍️

This weekend market brings together influencers, artists, makers and everyday sellers at River City Bangkok, with more than 50 booths of pre-loved clothing, vintage finds, collectibles, handmade pieces and original work.

Whether you are hunting for a wardrobe refresh or a one-off piece that nobody else has, it is worth a proper rummage. 

Once you are done shopping, wander the galleries and art exhibitions dotted throughout @rivercitycontemporary. 

Catch it on the first floor of River City Bangkok on Saturday and Sunday June 27-28, from midday. Entry is free.
