At the end of this year Bangkok turns itself over to imagination once more, with a festival that gathers everyone to celebrate the thrill of live performance 👩‍🎨🖼🎨

The @bangkok_theatre_festival hits its 24th year, throwing open a space where artists from every discipline meet, swap ideas and show their work to audiences from November 14-29. It's a proper coming-together, no airs and graces.

This time round the festival keeps company with old friends like the Bangkok Art and Culture Centre (BACC) and TK Park, while stretching out to fresh venues backed by @t.namcharoen.playhouse and @taisiam.official. Different dimensions, different surprises – for the makers and the watchers alike. See you there 🎭
