Right now, the trend of plant-based eating and sustainable living is becoming increasingly popular among urban people. Today, we would like to introduce a place that brings together plant-based products, a cafe, and an event space. Overall, it caters to city people who want to live a more sustainable lifestyle. This place is called @km6_bkk.

To begin with, the EKM6 project was created from the idea of building a relaxing space for city residents. The word ‘EKM’ does not only stand for Ekkamai, but also means ‘Every Kind Moment.’ The number 6 at the end refers to its location in Ekkamai Soi 6.

If you’re looking for a laid-back community mall that’s suitable for relaxation and escaping the chaos, this place is a great fit. The intention behind EKM6 is not only to focus on selling plant-based products; it is also designed as a space where people can rest and feel closer to nature. You can experience sunlight, breeze, natural sounds and relaxing on a grassy lawn – all of this is possible in Ekkamai Soi 6.
