Issara (@issara.phuket) is Phuket’s newest obsession. 

A restaurant that brings together two brothers – a MasterChef-winning chef and an accomplished mixologist – both sharing a love for hospitality and honest good food. Over the past decade, the island has shaped their story, while travels across Asia – particularly Japan – continue to inspire their approach to food, drinks and design.

The result is a restaurant that's deeply personal. Every cocktail, every dish, every song on the playlist and every corner of the space has been carefully crafted by the brothers themselves, blending European technique with Asian influences to create an experience that's as memorable as it is delicious.

Swipe through to meet the people behind Issara and tap their page to book yourself a seat. It’s so worth it…
