Bangkok Art Biennale 2026 is rolling back into town, and this one is built around a theme with plenty of bite.

A fresh wave of artists from Thailand and around the world has joined the city's sprawling contemporary art festival, which returns across temples, museums, malls and major landmarks from October 26 2026 to February 28 2027.

This year’s theme is 'Angels & Mara' – angels and demons, light and dark, hope and despair, virtue and temptation. Expect artists poking at belief, power, identity and the blurry borders of being human right now.

More names, shows and public programmes will land closer to opening. For now, start plotting your route.
