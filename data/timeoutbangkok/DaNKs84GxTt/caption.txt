Cool art might come from the worst nights 🌙 

Pillow, the debut solo show from Sittha Jantharawong, better known as @hommes.hom, gathers up the small sleepless hours most of us would rather forget and turns them gently inward. A restless mind. Insomnia. That little bottle of pills on the nightstand. He makes a sanctuary of the lot. 

Funny that he's a former adland creative, trained to clock how people behave and what they hide. He brings that same eye to the feelings we bury deepest. 

Catch him in conversation on Saturday July 11, 5.30-7.40pm. 

The exhibition runs July 9 to August 23 at MMAD Gallery 1, MunMun Srinakarin. Free entry.
