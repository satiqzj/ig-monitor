Singapore’s best-known independent shopping event is finally crossing the border 🛍️ 

After more than 20 years championing emerging labels through @boutiquefairssg, founder Charlotte Cain brings the whole thing to Thailand for the very first time. Boutiques Asia: The Bangkok Edition 2026 takes over the 7th floor, ICONSIAM from July 24-26.

More than 120 brands from Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia, Hong Kong, South Korea and Japan, all under one roof across three days. Fashion, accessories, lifestyle, travel goods. Making their Bangkok debut are @wnwhitenoise, the Indonesian label crafting travel gear from Japan-certified recycled nylon; @rocketeyewear’s polarised frames made for Asian facial features; @anayalife’s natural leather-care collection; Singapore favourite @grayestudio, whose clean-lined clothes genuinely hold up; and @sojaoshop, purveyor of GOTS-certified organic cotton bedding, towels and loungewear .

Ready to shop? 🎟️ Tickets start at B160 for a single day or B450 for all three. Kids under 12 get in free, and KTC and SCB CardX holders save 25 percent off admission.
