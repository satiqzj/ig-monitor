Thinking of retiring in Thailand? The numbers are on your side.

Thailand has been named Asia’s best place to retire in International Living’s 2026 Global Retirement Index, climbing to ninth worldwide and overtaking Malaysia for the regional top spot.

The kingdom scored 80 out of 100 overall, with cost of living leading the way at 96. Development and governance followed at 84, while healthcare and visas and retiree benefits both scored 79.
