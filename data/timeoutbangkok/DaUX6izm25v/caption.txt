Check the line-up, then grab your ticket and get ready to rock at @monstermusicfest 🎤🎸🎶

As the biggest music festival in the heart of Bangkok, it gathers every scene under one roof. Indie, pop, hard rock, whatever you're after, this is where you live the music from the first light of morning right through to the last beat of the night.

Big Ass, Bowky Lion, Ink Waruntorn, Jeff Satur, Taitosmith, Three Man Down and YOUNGOHM headline. And that's just a slice of the two-day bill, with over a hundred more acts and a stack of surprises still under wraps 🔥
