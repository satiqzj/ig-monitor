Prime Video's new shark thriller The Devil's Mouth turns Thailand's caves and coastlines into the setting for a survival nightmare with very little breathing room – and it was shot right here, over eight weeks across Krabi province, Chiang Mai and Bangkok.

Directed by Jeff Wadlow, whose credits include  Imaginary and Truth or Dare, the film follows five college friends on one last trip together before adult life gets in the way. Their guided swim through a remote cave system known as The Devil's Mouth takes a turn when they discover a freak storm has flooded the caves with sea life. Most of it died in the fresh water. One bull shark did not.

Kathryn Newton, Lana Condor, Gavin Casalegno, Nico Hiraga and Tommi Rose play the friends, while Thai actor Tayme Thapthimthong, fresh from The White Lotus season 3, plays the guide who leads them into the caves.

The Devil's Mouth streams globally on Prime Video from July 29 2026.
