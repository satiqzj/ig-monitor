Fresh off its craft beer festival that swallowed the shophouse streets of Song Wat Road whole, Sip Thai by Song Craft swaggers back from July 6-12 at Parkside Hall, G floor of Dusit Central Park.

@songcraftfestivals is a community, championing Thai producers from every region and reframing the whole business as 'Liquid Art' – creativity, music and contemporary art in one glass.

You’ll meet quality producers from every region of Thailand gather under one roof. Knowledge talks let industry insiders spill the real stories behind the bottle. Every day brings DJs on the decks, live music takes over on July 10-11, and vendor promotions live and die within this one glorious week.

Free entry, 11am-10pm. Go on then 🍻 ✨
