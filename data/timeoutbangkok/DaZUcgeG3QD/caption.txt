It sold out and left everyone wanting more, so Ghibli Live at The Palace comes back for a Restage Concert 🎐

Here's the thing: those soundtracks aren't background music, they're childhood in a bottle. A live ensemble reworks the pieces you already know by heart – the wistful ones, the hopeful ones, the ones that make you go a bit soft – and plays them beneath the frankly stunning architecture of Thewarat Sapharom Throne Hall. Music, art and heritage, all in one room 👒

Five shows run January 8 2027 at 7pm, then January 9-10at 2pm and 7pm at the Thewarat Sapharom Throne Hall, Phyathai Palace. Tickets drop July 6, 10am.
