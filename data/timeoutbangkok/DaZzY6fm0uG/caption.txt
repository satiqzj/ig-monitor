When the first buds show up, Hua Takhe comes alive right alongside them 💐

All month the old riverside market dresses itself in blooms for Rakdok Floral Weeks 2026, a flower fair running under the theme 'Flower to Spread Smiles'. Take your time, wander the lanes and the charm of this historic little community shows itself through petals and art.

20 floral installations scatter about the place, joined by workshops, craft sessions and plenty of creative bits to have a go at. And it's not just pretty things to photograph. Stories hide here too, with a smile or two waiting to be found.

🤳✨ Bring your camera and spend a lovely day among the flowers at Hua Takhe Old Market, July 4-August 2, 10am-6pm. Free entry.
