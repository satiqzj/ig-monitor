Some songs you listen to. This one you walk through.

@pun___official’s ‘Museum of Somnia’ is an embodied music experience built entirely around the music of one of Thailand’s most promising voices, known for his intimate storytelling and genre-blending sound. 

Running July 25 to August 8 at centralwOrld LIVE, each room is a different chapter of life – joy, longing, hope, farewell – and no two visits are the same: every 60-minute slot randomly lands you in one of three seasons, Summer, Rain or Autumn-Winter.

You won’t know which until you’re inside.
