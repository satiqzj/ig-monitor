The world spins faster every day and technology keeps sprinting ahead of us all. Fancy slowing things down for an afternoon? 

@thebitstudio is the first permanent gallery to set up shop on the third floor of @slowcombo, and it opens with a debut show that marries cutting-edge tech and art in the most captivating way.

The whole thing is immersive and interactive, so the wall between you and the work simply dissolves. Reach out, touch, play, become part of the art itself.

After touring more than 25 countries, the team lands in Thailand with four distinct rooms that wake up all five senses. 

Open Friday, Saturday and Sunday, 2pm till 8pm. Tickets are B450, B200 for students and free for under-12s with a grown-up 🎟️
