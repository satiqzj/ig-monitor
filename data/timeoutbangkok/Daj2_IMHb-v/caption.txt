Bangkok's oldest riverside quarter puts on its evening face again as Awakening Song Wat returns for a second year ✨🏮 

Stay out late and Soi Song Wat, Charoen Watthana Warehouse and a handful of tucked-away old buildings glow with 21 lighting installations and digital works across 14 spots. 

This year's theme, 'SON(G)EVITY', asks what keeps the place ticking through the generations. 
Free entry, July 3-12, 6pm-11pm. Follow @awakeningfestivals and @fridaybkk.th for the map and programme before you set off.
