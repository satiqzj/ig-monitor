Another Thai name takes the world stage 🎤 Phuket teenager @neneroyalmusic performs 'Zombie' by The Cranberries on America's Got Talent, and all four judges say yes. She's 16, she writes her own songs and she sails through to the next round.

The room stands. The judges praise her musicianship, then the charisma and swagger they reckon a rock star needs. 

Before the lights and the buzzers, she busks. Evenings and weekends across Phuket's markets, guitar case open on the concrete, amp running off a borrowed extension lead, singing over woks, motorbikes and tourists rifling through racks of vests.

Global superstardom next, apparently. Watch this space ⭐
