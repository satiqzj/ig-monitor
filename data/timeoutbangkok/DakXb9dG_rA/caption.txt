This week’s food news comes with birthdays, seasonal ingredients and a few unexpected twists. LARDER (@larderbkk) marks four years with KA NAP KI, its chef takeover series bringing some of Bangkok’s best restaurants into its kitchen. Akira Back Bangkok (@akirabackbkk) goes hard on summer truffles from France and Italy, while Summer Palace (@intercontinentalbangkok) puts grouper in the spotlight with a Cantonese menu built around the prized fish. Song Wat gets a new upstairs hangout with Ciao SongWat (@ciao.songwat), and 25 Degrees (@25degreesinbangkok) gives burger lovers two limited reasons to drop by: a Japanese A5 wagyu burger and a thick Hershey milkshake.
