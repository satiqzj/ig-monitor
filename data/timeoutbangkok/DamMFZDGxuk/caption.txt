Night owls with library cards, this one's for you. 📚 

The Goethe-Institut Thailand Library now stays open till 8pm on the first Wednesday of every month, a new ritual it calls Late Night Library. It kicks off Wednesday, July 8 and runs through December, so you get six goes at making it a habit. From Surasak BTS it's a ten-minute stroll. 

Chat away, practise your German, huddle up with friends. Snacks are welcome too, provided they don't smell and your drink has a lid. Free for everyone, students or not. 

The rest of the week keeps normal hours – 9.30am weekdays, 8am-5pm Saturdays, 8.30am-4.30pm Sundays.
