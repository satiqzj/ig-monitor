Some dogs win a city by being fearless. Wednesday, the little brown dachshund who has charmed Bangkok by being anything but, now gets her biggest show yet 🐾

'Wednesday: All Over the Place' arrives at River City Bangkok this July, following one anxious sausage dog through fear, self-doubt, strange places and the small bravery of keeping going anyway.

Created by illustrator and contemporary pop artist Newyear, the exhibition unfolds across five zones, from looping thoughts to stepping outside your comfort zone. And for the first time, River City Bangkok is welcoming pets too, as long as they stay tucked in a stroller or carrier. 🖼️

Catch it at RCB Galleria 2, July 9-September 6, daily 10am-8pm. Tickets are B100, or B150 with your pet.
