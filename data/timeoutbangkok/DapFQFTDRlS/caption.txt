Calling all retro lovers and collectors, a newly renovated community mall just opened in Ramkhamhaeng and it is a vintage haven you don’t want to miss. 

1981 Soul&Sold comes in a ‘Newstalgia’ concept blending old retro charms with modern lifestyle needs.

Housed in an industrial-style building, there is everything vintage enthusiasts can ever want and more with vintage clothing, handmade crafts, collectibles, paintings, home decor as well as tattoo parlors, bars and restaurants. 

Shopping and eating aside, there are also interactive activities like live DJ’s and art spaces making it a place you can spend a whole day at and still want to revisit.

1981 Soul&Sold is located at The Mall Ramkhamhaeng, Soi 15 and is open daily from 11am-11pm.
