Our deepest condolences go out to the families, friends and loved ones of those who lost their lives, and we wish a full and speedy recovery to everyone who was injured.

A fire broke out at a venue in Bangkok’s Lad Phrao district in the early hours of July 12, resulting in a tragic loss of life and leaving many others injured. It is one of the most devastating venue fires in Thailand in recent years.

As a publication that champions the best of Bangkok, we believe everyone should be able to enjoy a night out knowing the venues they visit are safe. We hope the authorities are able to determine the cause of this tragedy through a thorough investigation, and that any lessons learned lead to stronger safety measures to help prevent such a devastating incident from happening again.

Our thoughts remain with all those affected.
