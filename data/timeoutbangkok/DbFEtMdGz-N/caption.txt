Miss the thrill of rummaging through a rooftop market until your feet ache? Good news, Tay Level 10 returns for another round. 

More than 80 vintage sellers, craft makers, home décor stalls, fashion rails and street food vendors take over the 10th-floor car park of S-Oasis in Chatuchak, that sweet spot where the everyday and the treasure hunt collide. 

Rifle through secondhand gems, film cameras, leafy plants or a bit of shiny arm candy, then reward yourself with something delicious once the clock strikes home time. And it's a doddle to reach, sitting pretty by the MRT and BTS. 

At S-Oasis, near Chatuchak Park MRT and Mo Chit BTS. Free entry. August 6-8, midday-8pm.
