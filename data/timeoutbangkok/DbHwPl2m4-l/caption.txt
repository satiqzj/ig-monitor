Books, peace and a little cat supervision 🐈📚

Inside Wat Pak Nam in Phasi Charoen, Mongkolsiri Library holds shelves of non-fiction, novels, translated titles and rare finds – plus cats that treat the place as their own. 

It comes as Bangkok leans further into its bookish side, with Bangkok Book District Fest returning to Phra Nakhon on July 25-26. 

Entry is free. Open daily 9.30am-4pm, except public holidays, near BTS Bang Wa or MRT Bang Phai.
