Explore Talat Noi and Charoen Krung, one delicious flavour at a time 🍜🍽️

At Talat Noi-Charoen Krung, wander its narrow lanes and you find food, art and community stories layered on top of one another, each corner offering a small reward for anyone willing to look a little closer.

This is where you get acquainted with the locals who make the district tick, from Charmkrung and Tae Lao Chin Seng to Kway Teow Roo Talat Noi, Rice9 Gelato Shop, Sunset Coffee Roasters, Envies, Jittawilai Photo Studio and many more.

You check in as a Charoen CITYZEN, collect your CITYZEN ID and journal, then set off on your own adventure. Along the way you hunt for stamps and hidden pockets of the quarter, browse the mini gallery ‘Served with Art’ at Charmgall and stumble across DJ pop-ups that turn up when you least expect them. Fancy a flutter? The Charoen CITYZEN lucky draw hands special prizes to a few fortunate souls.

Every activity comes free. You start at @thecornerhouse.bangkok and roam the whole neighbourhood from there. Mark your diary for July 24-30 (Mondays off), and show up any time between 11am and 7pm.
